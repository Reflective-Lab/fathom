# Fathom SPARC Knowledge Base

Convergence-driven analysis of public-company financial filings.

This KB exists so the app can grow quickly as a real marquee application while feeding reusable pressure back into Bedrock, Mosaic, Organism, Axiom, Converge, and Helms.

## App Boundary

This app owns:

- financial-filing vocabulary, analyst questions, and report semantics
- SEC/EDGAR fixtures, filing examples, and finance-domain truth catalogs
- domain suggestor posture for filing risk, segment truth, disclosure drift, and analyst-facing evidence
- product decisions about what an analyst can inspect, trust, challenge, and export

This app consumes or should route to:

- Converge for deterministic promotion, disagreement, provenance, and HITL gates
- Organism for reusable formation composition across filing, portfolio, and M&A questions
- Prism for professional regression, fuzzy inference, ranking, forecasting, anomaly detection, and analytic critique
- Ferrox for portfolio optimization, feasibility, and allocation questions when needed
- Arbiter, Manifold, Embassy, and Mnemos when policy, providers, connectors, or memory become reusable concerns

## Rule

When a capability looks reusable, do not deepen local machinery. Capture the product pressure here, route it to the correct platform owner, and keep only the smallest temporary shim needed to keep the app moving.
